User Safety and Guidelines

To ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone, please follow these guidelines:

  1. Be Respectful: Treat everyone with kindness and respect. No bullying, harassment, or hate speech.
  2. Stay Authentic: Be true to yourself but mindful of others' feelings. Avoid sharing false information or impersonating others.
  3. Protect Privacy: Never share personal information like addresses, phone numbers, or financial details.
  4. Report Misconduct: If you encounter inappropriate behavior, report it immediately. Help us maintain a safe community.
  5. Have Fun: Show off your sense of humor, unleash your creativity, and enjoy connecting with friends in a positive way.

By following these guidelines, we can all ensure a safe and fun environment for everyone!
